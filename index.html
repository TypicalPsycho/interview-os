Interview.OS

A structured AI operating system for executive interview judgment, role-fit assessment, and signal calibration.

Interview.OS is designed to help senior leaders evaluate opportunities more clearly—before, during, and after interviews—by surfacing risk, alignment, and decision signals that are often missed or misinterpreted.

The public GPT is a limited, early-access interface into Interview.OS. It exposes a small subset of the system’s thinking and evaluation approach, intended for exploration and feedback rather than full capability representation. The broader platform is under controlled development and evaluation.
